ECMAtlas, An Introduction...

Biochemical characterization of ECM proteins has been hampered due to many ECM proteins resistance to standard sample preparation methods.

As a result, analyses of ECM proteins by traditional proteomic techniques have proven to be challenging.

One goal of the site is to a facilitate method development efforts by providing a platform to compare results from various ECM analysis experiments.
